Resources

This page contains a list of all the courses I've taken at the University of Alberta. You may find the description for each of these courses in the University of Alberta course catalogue. Please keep in mind that the difficulty and review I provide is subjective to my personal experiences immediately after finishing each course; your mileage may vary. I do not intend to rate the professors out of respect for them. You may find subjective ratings for professors at a website like Rate My Professors.


For reference, my difficulty scale is out of 5 with the following descriptors:


  • 1: Minimal applications, minimal tricky concepts, minimal tricky questions.
  • 2: Some applications, minimal tricky concepts, occasional tricky question.
  • 3: Some applicatios, occasional tricky concepts, some tricky questions.
  • 4: Lots of application, some tricky concepts, some tricky questions.
  • 5: Lots of application, lots of tricky concepts, lots of tricky questions.
